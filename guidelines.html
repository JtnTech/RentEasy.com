Guidelines & Penalty Policy

RentEasy thrives on the trust and responsibility of the student community. To ensure all items rotate fairly among students needing them, strict penalty guidelines are enforced for late returns.

warning Late Return Policy

If any student does not return the product after their rental period (a semester or a month) ends, they will be liable to pay a 2% daily penalty calculated on the original retail price of the item.

account_balance College Action

Failure to return the item and pay the associated penalties will result in a formal report being submitted to the College Administrative Office. Academic registration holds or disciplinary actions may be applied by the college until dues are cleared.

security Loss or Damage

If an item is lost or severely damaged beyond usable condition, the student must pay the remaining balance of the original retail price. The security deposit will be forfeited.
